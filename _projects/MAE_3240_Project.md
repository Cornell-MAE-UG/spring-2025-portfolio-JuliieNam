This was the first homework assignment for the heat transfer class that I took at Cornell:
[Heat Transfer Homework Assignment]({{ "/assets/3240 HW 1.pdf" | relative_url }})

The 3 dominant heat trasnfer modes (conduction, convection, and radiation) is something that I learned for my thermodynamics class, but in this class, we are going into more depth into these 3 modes. Therefore, these show up in almost every homework assignment. This homework was a nice way to refresh my memories on the 3 modes and also helped me learn more about how these could be applied to real life. For example, the third question of this homework helped me analyze whether the roof temperature would be too hot for a cat to work on, and the use of diagrams and equations helped me visualize what kind of heat transfer is going on. This homework assignment helped set up the foundation of what I will learn in this course.
